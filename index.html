How to Use

Getting Started

  • Click "Add Task" to create a new task
  • Choose task type: Homework, Exam, Quiz, or Personal
  • Fill in title, subject, due date/time, and priority
  • Click "Add Task" to save

Understanding Colors

  • Red border: Due within 24 hours (urgent!)
  • Orange border: Due within 3 days
  • Blue border: Due later than 3 days

Views

  • Dashboard: Shows tasks due within 24 hours
  • Pending: All incomplete tasks
  • Subjects: Filter by course/subject

Tips

  • Check Dashboard every morning
  • Use HIGH priority for exams and major projects
  • Keep subject names consistent
  • Your data is saved automatically in your browser
